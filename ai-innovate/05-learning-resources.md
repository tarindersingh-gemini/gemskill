### Essential Guides

**1. OpenAI Prompt Engineering Guide**
- Link: https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/prompt-engineering
- Official best practices

**2. Anthropic Prompt Library**
- Link: https://docs.anthropic.com/claude/prompt-library
- Curated prompts for various tasks

**3. Prompt Engineering Guide (GitHub)**
- Link: https://github.com/dair-ai/Prompt-Engineering-Guide
- Comprehensive community resource

### Techniques & Patterns

**Chain-of-Thought Prompting**
- Ask AI to show its reasoning step-by-step
- Example: "Let's think through this step by step..."

**Few-Shot Learning**
- Provide examples before asking for output
- Shows desired format and style

**Role Prompting**
- Assign a specific role/persona to the AI
- Example: "You are an expert business analyst..."

**Structured Output**
- Request specific format (JSON, table, list)
- Example: "Provide the answer in JSON format..."
